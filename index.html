OVERTHROWN!

Four teenagers — school friends and aspiring content creators — share a passion for livestreams, challenges, and spooky games. Among them is Eder, the creative spark always chasing the next big thrill. One day, he comes across a mysterious horror map — a place that "no one has ever finished." Rumor has it, the game is weird… and incredibly hard. “Probably fake… but sounds like epic content!” Eder says, and convinces his friends to try a “new format”: explore a haunted mansion in-game and record everything. At first, it looks like any other horror map... But soon, they start to realize something is wrong.
